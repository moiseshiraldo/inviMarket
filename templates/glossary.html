Glossary

C

  • Complaint

    The act of requesting the admin to review a trade due to unsent invites.

O

  • Offer

    The act of offering an invite to a specific website.

P

  • Partner

    A user you can trust to send and receive invites.

  • Popularity

    An evaluation of the users interest in a site, based on the number of requests, offers and visits.

R

  • Rating

    An evaluation of the user activity based on positive actions, such as donating invites or updating sites information. The rating is calculated with the following formula:

    Rating = 4×donations + 2×trades + 2×sites + editions − 5×warnings − 2×inactivity

    Where inactivity represents the number of months since the user's last visit.

  • Referral chain

    A chain of users where a new user sign up for the target site using the referral link of the last one.

  • Referral link

    A link to register in a site, which gives the referral, and sometimes the new registered user as well, some sort of reward.

  • Request

    The act of asking for an invite to a specific website.

T

  • Trade

    An exchange of invites between two users or a donation from one to another.

W

  • Warning

    A notice given to an user due to an accepted or automatic complaint when the invites have not been sent. After three warnings the user will be banned.
