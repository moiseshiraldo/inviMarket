Glossary

O

  • Offer

    The act of offering an invite to a specific website.

P

  • Partner

    A user you can trust to send and receive invites.

  • Popularity

    An evaluation of the users interest in a site, based on the number of requests, offers and visits.

R

  • Rating

    An evaluation of the user activity based on positive actions, such as donating invites or updating sites information.

  • Referral link

    A link to register in a site, which gives the referral, and sometimes the new registered user as well, some sort of reward.

  • Request

    The act of asking for an invite to a specific website.

T

  • Trade

    An exchange of invites between two users or a donation from one to another.
